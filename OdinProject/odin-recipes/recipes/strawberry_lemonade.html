Black Tea Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 2 tbs white sugar
  • 8 strawberries
  • 2 cups lemon juice
  • 1 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Place strawberries in blender, top with 2 tbs sugar and 1 cup of water. Blend until smooth
  2. Transfer blend to pitcher.
  3. Add 6 cupus of water, lemon juice and 1 cup of sugar.
  4. Pour into a jug and chill in fridge.
