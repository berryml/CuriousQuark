Watermelon Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice

Directions

  1. In a blender, puree watermelon cubes until smooth. Strain through a fine mesh sieve.
  2. Add sugar to 1/2 cup water and boil until sugar dissolves.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in 3 cups of cold water and the lemon juice.
