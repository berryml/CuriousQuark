Black Tea Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 5 cups water
  • 7 tea bags
  • 1 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Boil 5 cups of water.
  2. Add sugar and mix until dissolved.
  3. Add teabags and bring back to a boil.
  4. Steep for 7-10 minutes, depending on desired strength.
  5. Discard tea bags.
  6. Add lemon juice and stir.
  7. Chill in refrigerator.
